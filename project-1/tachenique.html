STRONGEST TECHNIQUES OF STRONGEST BEINGS

Sorcerers (15)

Canon-inspired technique summaries — short, neutral descriptions for quick reference.

YU
Yuji Itadori
Grade: 1 / Cursed Energy: High (physical focus)
  • Divergent Fist — Powerful strike with delayed shockwave.
  • Black Flash — Momentary spatial distortion that amplifies damage.
  • Enhanced Physicals — Exceptional speed, reflexes and stamina.
Straightforward close-range brawler who mixes martial skill with raw cursed-energy bursts.
ME
Megumi Fushiguro
Grade: Special/Technique: Ten Shadows
  • Ten Shadows Technique — Summons shikigami for offense, defense and utility.
  • Divine Dogs / Nue — Versatile summons for tracking and multi-target attacks.
  • Domain Potential — Shown to have strong domain amplification potential.
Tactical summoner who controls battles with flexible shikigami combinations.
NO
Nobara Kugisaki
Grade: 1 / Technique: Straw Doll Manipulation
  • Straw Doll Technique — Uses nails and a straw doll to connect and inflict damage remotely.
  • Resonance — Links target and doll to transfer harm.
  • Close Combat — Uses hammer and nails for precise strikes.
Elegant and brutal — combines ranged curses with surgical melee attacks.
GO
Satoru Gojo
Grade: Special / Technique: Limitless & Six Eyes
  • Limitless — Manipulates infinity at range for defense and control.
  • Infinity — Passive barrier that slows/blocks incoming attacks.
  • Hollow Purple — Devastating combined technique that erases matter along its path.
Top-tier sorcerer whose spatial techniques redefine offense and defense.
KN
Kento Nanami
Grade: Special / Technique: Ratio Cuts
  • Ratio Technique — Creates weak points at a fixed ratio for precise lethal strikes.
  • Professional Tactics — Efficient cursed-energy management and battlefield control.
Analytical fighter who turns calculation into deadly precision.
MA
Maki Zenin
Grade: 1 / Weapon Specialist
  • Weapon Mastery — Expert with cursed tools and close-quarters combat.
  • Physical Prowess — High strength and combat reflexes; low innate cursed energy.
    • \
Relies on training and cursed tools to compensate for limited raw energy.
TO
Toge Inumaki
Grade: 1 / Cursed Speech
  • Cursed Speech — Spoken commands imbued with cursed energy force compliance or cause harm.
  • Whisper Control — Uses minimal phrases and code words to avoid collateral damage.
High-risk, high-control technique; must limit speech to avoid hurting allies.
PA
Panda
Grade: 1 / Cursed Corpse Variant
  • Multiple Cores — Different cores grant unique forms and abilities.
  • Durability & Strength — Naturally high resilience and combat adaptability.
Unexpectedly versatile — a living cursed corpse with distinct tactical roles.
YU
Yuta Okkotsu
Grade: Special / Technique: Rika (Cursed Spirit Bond)
  • Rika Bond — Massive cursed energy reservoir through a bound spirit.
  • Versatile Arsenal — Can manifest a wide range of offensive and support abilities via Rika.
Extremely high energy ceiling thanks to a bonded cursed spirit; versatile on the battlefield.
AT
Aoi Todo
Grade: 1 / Technique: Boogie Woogie
  • Boogie Woogie — Instantaneous positional swap via cursed energy rhythm.
  • Brute Strength — Exceptional raw power in close quarters.
Unpredictable fighter whose swapping technique upends enemy formations.
SH
Shoko Ieiri
Grade: Support / Healing: Reverse Cursed Technique
  • Reverse Cursed Technique — Heals by converting cursed energy into life-restoring output.
  • Medical Expertise — Combat triage and post-battle treatment specialist.
Primary medical sorcerer, crucial for keeping teams in fighting shape.
KM
Kasumi Miwa
Grade: 2 / Technique: Swordsmanship with Cursed Energy
  • Swordplay Enhancement — Channels cursed energy through blade techniques.
  • Supportive Tactics — Combines offense with close escort roles.
Reliable team fighter who augments weapon strikes with cursed energy.
UI
Utahime Iori
Grade: 2 / Technique: Barrier & Support
  • Barrier Techniques — Defensive constructs for protection and area control.
  • Support Casting — Tactical bolstering of allies in the field.
Support sorcerer skilled in area control and defensive magic.
MM
Mei Mei
Grade: 1 / Technique: Tactical Sniping
  • Long-range Cursed Attacks — Precision and control at distance.
  • Contract Negotiation — Skilled at information and resource leverage.
Cold, efficient, and ruthlessly pragmatic — a long-range specialist.
SG
Suguru Geto
Grade: Special / Technique: Curse Manipulation
  • Curse Control — Absorbs and commands other curses as tools or minions.
  • Mass Manipulation — Large-scale influence over cursed entities.
Powerful and ideological sorcerer with techniques aimed at controlling curses themselves.

Cursed Spirits (15)

Summaries of notable cursed spirits and their signature techniques.

SU
Ryomen Sukuna
King of Curses / Vast Cursed Energy
  • Dismantle / Cleave — Slashing techniques that adjust to target properties.
  • Domain Expansion: Malevolent Shrine — Extremely lethal, area-targeted technique.
  • Innate Territory — Overwhelming cursed-energy output and variety.
A top-tier, ancient cursed spirit with a vast repertoire and near-unmatched power.
MA
Mahito
Transfiguration / Soul Manipulation
  • Idle Transfiguration — Alters a target's soul shape to deform or kill.
  • Soul Multiplicity — Creates variations and combinations by manipulating souls.
Dangerous because his techniques directly affect minds and bodies at a soul level.
JO
Jogo
Volcano-themed Cursed Spirit / Fire Manipulation
  • Fiery Projectiles — High-temperature ranged attacks and explosions.
  • Domain Expansion: Coffin of the Iron Mountain — Overwhelming area fire attack.
Powerful long-range burner with terrain-altering abilities.
HA
Hanami
Nature-themed / Plant Control
  • Plant Manipulation — Controls flora for offense, defense and area denial.
  • Regenerative Attributes — Uses nature-like resilience to recover or spread influence.
Uses natural motifs to attack and manipulate battlefields in unconventional ways.
DA
Dagon
Water / Sea-themed Cursed Spirit
  • Water Manipulation — Summons tides, creatures and domain-based drown attacks.
  • Domain Expansion — Large area control using water constructs.
Effects center on aquatic siege and area denial using massive spatial control.
CH
Choso
Cursed Blood / Brotherhood
  • Cursed Blood Techniques — Uses blood-based attacks and domain potential.
  • Resonant Abilities — Power linked to siblings and bloodline themes.
A cursed being with powerful blood techniques and surprising adaptability.
ES
Eso
Paired Cursed Spirit (with Kechizu)
  • Pair Assaults — Works with Kechizu for coordinated attacks.
  • High Agility — Fast and lethal close-quarter techniques.
Often encountered with Kechizu; their teamwork is the real threat.
KE
Kechizu
Paired Cursed Spirit (with Eso)
  • Pair Assaults — Complements Eso with lethal close-range brutality.
  • Predatory Tactics — Focus on dismemberment and incapacitation.
A brutal partner in duo attacks; surgically violent in close quarters.
FB
Finger Bearer
Cursed spirit variant / Often a vessel of Sukuna
  • Variable Skills — Techniques depend on origin; often dangerous in groups.
  • Sukuna-Linked Traits — Some fragments carry Sukuna's influence.
Common cursed puppet-type enemies; still dangerous depending on ancestry.
SB
Shadow Cursed (generic)
Shadow-based anomalies
  • Stealth / Shadow Blend — Ambush and infiltration focused.
  • Corrosive Touch — Weakens cursed-energy defenses over time.
Represents shadow types that emphasize stealth and attrition rather than raw force.
RS
Rust-type Cursed Spirit
Decay and corrosive motif
  • Corrosion Waves — Degrades objects and armor; dangerous over time.
  • Area Contamination — Creates zones hostile to sorcerers.
Uses slow-burn battlefield control and area denial to wear enemies down.
MS
Miasma Spirit
Toxic / Psychological attacks
  • Toxic Miasma — Produces poisonous fogs that disrupt minds and bodies.
  • Hallucinatory Effects — Causes confusion or fear to exploit openings.
Attacks weaker foes through sustained environmental and psychological pressure.
GV
Grave Vessel
Bound spirit / Heavy damage output
  • Heavy Burden — Strong single-target strikes with high durability.
  • Earth Manipulation — Can reshape terrain to trap or crush foes.
Slow but devastating — favors crushing tactics and chokepoints.
